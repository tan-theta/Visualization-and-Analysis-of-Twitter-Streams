Extension

Projecting the spread of sentiment across a variety of tweets from the datasets. The visualization technique used is a map that is shaded with a distribution of colors based on a range of a particular feature. In this implementation, that feature is the sentiment and the range of the sentiment goes from very negative to very positive. The impact of using a choropleth map is to highlight insights of sentiment across the geographical map such as if the regions on the map that have the highest cluster of very positive or very negative sentiment.
